An environment to help understand the science behind first impressions. Considerable research has shown a consensus in identifying babyfaced individuals at all ages, as well as a strong expectation that babyfaced people will have childlike traits-submissive, dependent, warm, affectionate, honest, weak, and naive. Although this babyface stereotype has been well documented, the question of its accuracy remains.
